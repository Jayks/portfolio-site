Case Study 1: Enterprise Data Platform Modernization
Client: Global Foodservice Distribution Leader
Role: Engagement Lead / Delivery Oversight

Business Challenge

Legacy and siloed data systems prevented a unified view of customers and operations.

Limited ability to predict and prevent customer churn in a competitive distribution market.

Slow data processing restricted real-time operational decision-making across supply chain and sales teams.

Solution Delivered

Led the modernization of the enterprise data platform and analytics ecosystem.

Built a cloud-native data lake on Amazon Web Services consolidating enterprise datasets.

Enabled advanced analytics and machine learning models to detect churn signals and customer trends.

Delivered integrated analytics for supply chain visibility and commercial performance insights.

Impact

Enabled predictive customer retention insights for proactive engagement.

Improved operational visibility across distribution and supply chain networks.

Established a scalable enterprise data platform supporting large-scale analytics workloads.

Case Study 3: Enterprise BI Modernization – Tableau to Power BI Migration
Client: Global Insurer
Role: Program Lead / Delivery Oversight

Business Challenge

The organization had ~1000 dashboards built on Tableau, resulting in high licensing and infrastructure costs.

Limited integration with the Microsoft analytics ecosystem, making collaboration and governance difficult.

Inconsistent data models and duplicated logic across dashboards caused maintenance challenges and reporting inconsistencies.

Business teams required scalable, governed, and cost-efficient BI capabilities.

Solution Delivered

Led the enterprise migration program to transition dashboards from Tableau to Microsoft Power BI.

Developed a migration framework including dashboard inventory, complexity classification, and automated conversion accelerators.

Standardized data models, semantic layers, and reporting templates to ensure consistency.

Implemented governance and security controls integrated with the Microsoft ecosystem.

Established agile delivery pods to accelerate migration across multiple business units.

Impact

Successfully migrated ~1000 dashboards to Power BI with minimal disruption to business users.

Reduced BI licensing and operational costs through platform consolidation.

Improved report performance, scalability, and user adoption.

Enabled centralized governance and standardized semantic models for enterprise reporting.

Delivered a future-ready analytics environment aligned with the Microsoft data platform.




Case Study 4: R&D Knowledge Digitization & Retrieval Platform
Client: Global Oil and Gas Company
Role: Engagement Lead / Analytics Program Oversight

Problem Statement

Critical R&D experiment results and chemical formulations were stored in physical documents and scanned PDFs, making access difficult.

Due to the lab environment, experiments were not digitally logged, resulting in fragmented information across multiple formats.

Absence of a centralized repository or single source of truth for experimental data.

Scientists often repeated experiments unknowingly, leading to wasted time, cost, and research effort.

Solution

Led the development of an automated R&D knowledge digitization platform to extract and structure historical research data.

Implemented OCR-based extraction to digitize experiment results and chemical formulation details from scanned documents and PDFs.

Built an entity mapping framework to structure experimental data, relationships, and research context.

Stored structured research knowledge in Neo4j graph database to capture relationships between compounds, experiments, and results.

Developed a web-based knowledge portal enabling researchers to search and retrieve experiment data.

Integrated an AI-powered chatbot interface to allow scientists to ask natural language questions about historical experiments and formulations.

Key Achievements

Digitized and structured previously inaccessible R&D knowledge from legacy documents.

Established a centralized knowledge repository for experimental research data.

Reduced duplicate experimentation and research effort by enabling experiment discovery.

Improved research efficiency and knowledge sharing across R&D teams.

Created a scalable knowledge graph foundation for future AI-driven scientific insights.



Case Study 5: Enterprise Knowledge Chatbot for Technical Documentation

Industry: Specialty Chemicals / Manufacturing
Role: Engagement Lead / Data Engineering & AI Solution Oversight

Problem Statement

Technical product information such as installation guides, maintenance manuals, instruction documents, and FAQs were stored across multiple formats and repositories.

Engineers and support teams struggled to quickly locate accurate information from large volumes of documentation.

Manual search across PDF, Word, and Excel files slowed troubleshooting and customer support processes.

The organization required a self-service knowledge access solution for employees and support teams.

Solution

Led the development of an enterprise knowledge chatbot platform using Microsoft Azure cloud architecture.

Built automated pipelines to parse and index technical documents including PDFs, Word files, Excel sheets, and FAQs.

Structured and ingested curated knowledge into QnA Maker to enable conversational question-answering capabilities.

Developed a chatbot interface that allowed users to retrieve answers through natural language queries.

Enhanced the system with NLP capabilities to improve intent detection and extract precise answers from technical documentation.

Key Achievements

Enabled conversational access to large volumes of technical documentation.

Reduced time required for engineers and support teams to locate relevant information.

Improved customer support efficiency and internal knowledge discovery.

Established an early enterprise chatbot capability prior to modern LLM-based solutions.

Created a scalable framework for AI-driven knowledge management and support automation.
